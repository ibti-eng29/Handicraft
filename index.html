Our Story

Crafting Excellence
Since 1972

Venus Handicrafts was founded in 1972 with a singular mission: to bring the timeless beauty of Indian artisanship to the global marketplace. From our origins as a modest workshop, we have grown into a certified manufacturing powerhouse, employing hundreds of master artisans whose skills have been passed down through generations.

Today, our products grace homes, hotels, galleries, and retail shelves across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. Every piece bears the hallmark of authentic craftsmanship — handcrafted, quality-inspected, and designed to endure.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything importers and wholesale buyers need to know before placing an order with Venus Handicrafts.

Still have questions?

Our export team is available Mon–Sat, 9 AM–6 PM IST. We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.

Contact Our Team

Our minimum order quantities vary by product category:

  • Iron & Aluminium Handicrafts: 50 pieces per design
  • Brass & Copper Products: 25–50 pieces per design
  • Wood Handicrafts: 50 pieces per design
  • Glass Handicrafts: 100 pieces per design
  • Gift & Festive Sets: 100 sets per design
  • For mixed containers, total order value should exceed USD 3,000

For first-time buyers, we offer sample orders (1–5 pieces) at retail prices plus courier charges, fully adjustable against bulk orders.

We ship worldwide using sea freight (FCL/LCL) and air freight depending on order size and urgency.

  • Sea Freight (USA/Europe): 25–35 days after dispatch
  • Sea Freight (Middle East/SE Asia): 15–25 days
  • Air Freight: 5–10 working days globally
  • Express Courier (DHL/FedEx): 3–7 working days for samples

We work on both FOB Moradabad/Jodhpur and CIF terms. All necessary export documentation is provided at no extra cost.

Venus Handicrafts holds the following certifications and industry memberships:

  • ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management Systems
  • Registered Export House — Government of India
  • FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations) Member
  • EPCH (Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts) Member
  • CE Marking compliance for EU-bound products
  • Prop 65 compliant finishes available for California importers

We can provide third-party lab test reports (SGS, Bureau Veritas) on request for specific product categories.

Absolutely. Custom design and private label manufacturing is a core part of our business. We offer:

  • Design development from buyer-supplied sketches or references
  • 3D rendering and sampling before bulk production
  • Custom colours, finishes, sizes, and packaging
  • Private label branding — swing tags, stickers, box printing
  • Exclusive design lock (not sold to other buyers)

Custom sample development typically takes 15–25 working days. A nominal tooling/sampling charge applies, which is 100% refundable against your first bulk order.

Production lead times depend on order size, product complexity, and seasonal capacity:

  • Small orders (under 500 pieces): 20–30 working days
  • Medium orders (500–2000 pieces): 30–45 working days
  • Large orders (2000+ pieces): 45–60 working days
  • Festive season orders (Sep–Nov): Add 15–20 extra days

We recommend placing festive season orders at least 90 days in advance. Rush production is available at a 15% surcharge for select product lines.

We offer flexible payment terms to suit different buyer relationships:

  • New buyers: 50% advance + 50% before shipment (T/T)
  • Regular buyers: 30% advance + 70% against BL copy
  • Established accounts: LC at sight (Letters of Credit)
  • PayPal / Credit Card accepted for sample orders (under USD 500)

All transactions are in USD, EUR, or GBP. We provide a formal Proforma Invoice before any payment is requested.

Our multi-stage quality inspection process ensures every shipment meets agreed specifications:

  • Raw material inspection at procurement stage
  • In-process quality checks at each production stage
  • Pre-shipment inspection of 100% cartons by our QC team
  • Third-party inspection (SGS/Bureau Veritas) available on request
  • Photo documentation and video walkthrough of packed goods

We maintain less than 1.5% defect rate across all product lines. Any defective goods discovered after arrival are replaced or credited in subsequent orders.
